A bit about me.

Howdy! I'm Greg, a 24 year young lad currently working in Rothco, a creative agency based in Dublin, Ireland. I'm a Digital Creative and part of the tech team. My work primarily consists of digital display. This can range from standard to rich and recently we've been looking at more dynamic and programatic creative.

I graduated from the Institue of Technology, Blanchardstown in 2015 with an honors degree in Creative Digital Media. Over the past couple of years I've worked on clients such as AIB, Tesco, Heineken, FAI and much more. I'm now at the stage where I'm looking to take the next step in my career and pursue a job as a front-end developer. I've done bits here and there in my current line of work but I want to be doing it full-time as a profession.

Work stuff aside, I like to spend my free time relaxing with the family (that's my Mam, two sisters, pet dog called Harvey and pet Lizard called Len). I love learning new things, no matter what it pertains to. I love a good book and I'm constantly listening to podcasts. I'm also a big football fan (GGMU!) and can never turn down a good gaming session.
