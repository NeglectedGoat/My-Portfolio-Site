CAMPAIGN: AIB THIRD LEVEL

THE PROJECT

A campaign for AIB to highlight their student plus account. This is probably my favorite piece of display work. It's very simple but I really like the illustrations and subtle animation. It was a challenging campaign as we were trying to tow the line of not having something too subtle but at the same time trying to ensure the animation didn't come across as jarring in any way. There was a lot of back and forth on this with the Art Director but in the end I'm really happy with how it turned out.
